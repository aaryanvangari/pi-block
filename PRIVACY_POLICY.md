# Privacy policy

The Pi Block provides functionality that works with privately controlled or otherwise accessible servers ("Third-party services") to interact with your Pi-Hole servers.
Your data is as secure as the Third-party Services you choose to store them on.

## Information you provide

**Account Information.** You provide urls and api tokens to your Pi-Hole servers.

**Data Collection.** Pi Block does not collect any personal data.
All information provided (Pi-Hole IP addresses and API keys) is stored locally on your device.
All app settings are cleared if you uninstall the application.

## Information Pi Block collects

Pi Block does not collect any data of any sorts.

## Information shared with others

Neither the authors of Pi Block nor the publisher receive any of the data you provide to Pi Block.
The app communicates directly with your Pi-Hole instance. No third-party tracking or analytics SDKs are used.

## Transparency

As an open-source project, you can verify how your data is handled by reviewing the source code on GitHub.
